BTS – Biography

Group Name: BTS

Full Form: Bangtan Sonyeondan (Bulletproof Boy Scouts)

Debut Year: 2013

Agency: Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE Corporation)

About BTS:

BTS is a South Korean boy band that debuted on June 13, 2013. The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.

Their music combines pop, hip-hop, R&B, and EDM styles. BTS is known for meaningful lyrics that focus on self-love, youth struggles, mental health, and social issues.

The group gained international fame with albums like "Love Yourself" and "Map of the Soul".

Achievements:

• First Korean group to top the Billboard Hot 100
• Multiple Billboard Music Awards winners
• Grammy nominations
• Global fanbase known as ARMY

"Love yourself, speak yourself."
